TAK Cesium Visualizer

This application provides a real-time 3D tactical view of Cursor-on-Target (CoT) data from a TAK Server.

Features

  • Real-time Tracking: Automated updates for ground, air, and sea assets.
  • Tactical Symbology: Support for MIL-STD-2525D icons, Public Safety Air icons, and GDACS natural disaster symbols.
  • Emergency Alerts: Automatic prioritization and visual highlighting of 911 alerts.
  • Filtering: Search by callsign, UID, or hashtag (#ais, #adsb, etc.).
  • Custom Map Layers: Integrated Finnish Topographic and Aerial imagery.

Navigation

  • Left Click: Select unit and view details.
  • Right Click + Drag: Tilt and rotate camera.
  • Middle Scroll: Zoom in and out.
  • Reset View: Orients camera North and straight down.
